Tech companies laying off 1,600 workers per day in 2023, and it's increasing, Jan 17, 2023

Over 1,600 employees are being fired per day in 2023 by tech companies globally, including in India, recent data has revealed. These mass layoff incidents are increasing across the globe amid fears about global recession and slowdown.
